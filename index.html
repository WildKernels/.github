Important Technical Information

Kernel vs Android Version: Your kernel version (e.g., 5.10.101-android12) may differ from your Android system version (e.g., Android 13). The kernel version typically corresponds to the Android version that shipped with your device.
GKI Evolution: GKI 2.0 (Android 12+) enforces a stable Kernel Module Interface, separating hardware-specific code into loadable modules, unlike Pre-GKI custom kernels that caused fragmentation.
LTS Support: All our kernels are based on Long-Term Support (LTS) kernel versions, ensuring stability and extended security updates for your device.
